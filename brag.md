# Brag Document

Running record of accomplishments in impact language, for use in performance
reviews, visibility conversations, and confidence calibration.

**Impact language rule:** "Shipped X which enabled Y to do Z" not "worked
on X." The test: could someone who doesn't know you read this and understand
why it matters?

Updated nightly by the evening agent from commits and meeting notes.
Synthesized quarterly before performance reviews.
